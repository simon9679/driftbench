# Research

Non-normative material that informs future versions of the DriftBench standard.
Nothing here is part of the frozen spec — refer to [`../standard/v1/`](../standard/v1/)
for the authoritative ontology and scenarios.

## Contents

- [`migration_matrix.md`](migration_matrix.md) — mapping from free-text research
  concepts to the canonical `core_id` values used by DriftBench Standard v1.

## In progress

Specifications for candidate v1.1 metrics — including a deterministic
**ambivalence** metric (sustained co-activation of conflicting beliefs), `OCS`
(oscillation calibration), and `TPS` (turning-point detection) — are being
drafted here. See [`../ROADMAP.md`](../ROADMAP.md).
